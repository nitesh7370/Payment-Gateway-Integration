FREE EDUCATION FOUNDATION

Free Education Foundation (FEF) is a Non-Profit Organization committed to support people all over the world by providing free online education. The main goal of our organization is to provide Education for All. Established in 2021, our organization partners with various educational institutions all across the globe to provide free and quality education.

The fundraiser Program

This fundraiser campaign is being organized by Free Education Foundation to make world class quality online courses for all of the people in the world. We have partnered with world class educational institutions to provide a set of courses on majors skills required in the field of Engineering, Science and Technology. When you donate and join the Free Education Foundation you get a chance to take up these courses and earn a verified certificate from Free Education. Foundation.
